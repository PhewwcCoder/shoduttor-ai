Balance and billing
If your balance was deducted unexpectedly, it may be due to a VAS subscription or background data usage. Dial *121*1*2# to check your current balance. To view active subscriptions dial *121*3*1#.

Internet and data packages
To check your remaining internet balance dial *121*1*4#. If your internet is not working, turn airplane mode on and off, then restart your phone. If the problem continues in your area, there may be a temporary network issue being resolved by our team.

Network issues
If you are experiencing poor signal or no network in your area, please note your exact location and the time. Our network team monitors outages and typically resolves local issues within 2-4 hours. You can report a network issue by calling 121.

Package activation
If your package did not activate after purchase, wait 5 minutes and check again by dialing *121*1*4#. If the package still does not show, call 121 with your transaction ID ready.

4G availability
4G coverage is available in all major cities including Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, and Rajshahi. Coverage in specific neighborhoods may vary. Dial *#*#4636#*#* to check your current network mode.
